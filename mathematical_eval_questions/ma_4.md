What proportion of planes in March contained caroges of 3.99 or less?

* 36.1%
* 44.6%
* 55.4%
* 62.8%

## Solution

* Total planes in March = 570
* Planes carrrying less than or equal to 3.99 tonnes = 127 + 127 = 254
* 254 / 570 x 100 = `44.6%`
