In 2021, what proportion of all hurricanes were 2 and 4?

* 24%
* 32%
* 38%
* 42%

## Solution

* Total hurricanes in 2021:
* 24 + 11 + 9 + 5 + 1 = 50

* Total category 2 and 4 hurricanes:
* 11 + 5 = 16

* Proportion of category 2 and 4 hurricanes:
* `(16 / 50) x 100 = 32%`
